TECHNOLOGY

Sensors

Our technology supports variety of sensors for measuring chemical and physical characteristics of air and water, as well as sonars, photo and video cameras. Actuators such as winches or gimbals could be used to control position and orientation of the sensors.

more...

Platforms

Platforms supply sensors and actuators with power, collect data from the sensors, and transfer the data to the data store. Mobile platforms also deliver sensors to the site.

more...

Data Processing

The data collected by the platforms are automatically processed and converted to the requested data formats and made available for download or as online maps.

more...

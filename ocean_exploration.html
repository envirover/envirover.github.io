Oceanographic Data Collection

Observations of physical and chemical characteristics of water and air in the requested areas of interest.

Water Quality

Conductivity, pH, ORP, dissolved oxygen, salinity, total dissolved solids, resistivity, density, and temperature

Waves

Wave height and wavelength

Acoustics

Sonar and hydrophone data

Air Quality

Temperature, barometric pressure, wind speed and direction

Search Missions

Searching for sources of contamination using sensor-based guidance.

Gradient Search

Searching for "hot-spots" of a certain parameters or source of contamination using sensor-based based guidance.

Scanning

Scanning the requested area of interest in orded to find a place where certain measured parameter is in a specified range.

Support of search and rescue missions

Search for survivors of aircraft downed at sea or sailors and passengers of sea vessels in distress.

Global Telemetry for Self-Driving Cars

Self-driving cars are just around the corner. Remote tracking, command, and control links for a fleet of autonomous cars will enable a wide range of fleet management applications.

Envirover is developing a satellite telemetry system for self-driving cars controlled by Apollo self-driving open software platform.
